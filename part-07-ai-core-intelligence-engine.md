## MEMORY IMPORTANCE SCORING

Every memory receives:
- **Importance Score** — Clinical relevance weight
- **Decay Score** — How much weight to give over time
- **Confidence Score** — AI certainty level
- **Recency Score** — How recent the information is
- **Clinical Relevance Score** — Applicability to current treatment

AI determines: Keep | Archive | Promote | Review
