## Egypt Launch Playbook (Month 1–6)

### Target Metrics
- 50 therapists signed up in Month 1
- 500 sessions in Month 1
- 200 therapists by Month 6
- $10K MRR by Month 6

### Channel 1: Direct Therapist Outreach

**Steps:**
1. Build list of 500 licensed therapists in Cairo + Alexandria
2. LinkedIn + email outreach with personalized demo invite
3. Demo → 14-day free trial → paid conversion
4. Referral ask at 30-day mark

**Message:** "Save 5 hours per week on documentation."

### Channel 2: University Partnerships

**Target:** Psychology departments, counseling centers

**Pitch:** Free tools for student therapists → creates referral network of trained professionals

**Universities:** Cairo University, AUC, Ain Shams, Helwan

### Channel 3: Mental Health Associations

**Targets:**
- Egyptian Society of Psychiatry
- Egyptian Psychologists Association
- Private practice networks

**Approach:** Sponsor events, speak at conferences, offer association member discounts

### Channel 4: Content Marketing

**Topics:**
- "How Egyptian therapists are saving 5 hours per week"
- "The future of mental health documentation in Egypt"
- "AI tools every therapist should know"

**Platform:** LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram (Arabic + English)

### Channel 5: Influencer/KOL Partnerships

**Target:** Mental health advocates, therapist influencers in Egypt/MENA

## MENA Expansion Playbook (Month 7–18)

### Market Priority Order
1. UAE (highest purchasing power, tech-forward)
2. Saudi Arabia (large market, Vision 2030 tailwinds)
3. Jordan (strong therapist community)
4. Kuwait / Qatar

### Key Differences per Market
- UAE: English-primary, international therapists
- Saudi Arabia: Arabic-primary, local compliance
- Jordan: Arabic-primary, price-sensitive

## Clinic Acquisition Playbook

### Target: Group Practices (3–15 therapists)

**Pain points:**
- Manual session notes
- No practice analytics
- Inconsistent documentation
- Billing complexity

**Sales motion:**
1. Identify practice manager / owner
2. Demo focused on admin efficiency
3. Start with 1 therapist pilot
4. Expand to full team in 30 days

## Referral System Design

### Therapist-to-Therapist Referral
- **Reward:** 2 months free per successful referral
- **Trigger:** After therapist completes 10 sessions
- **Mechanism:** Unique referral link, tracked conversions

### Patient Referral
- **Reward:** Session credit
- **Trigger:** After first session completion
- **Mechanism:** Share link with friend/family

## Growth Loops

### Loop 1: Therapist-Driven
```
Therapist signs up
 ↓ Uses AI Scribe
 ↓ Generates amazing notes
 ↓ Shares with colleagues
 ↓ Colleague signs up
```

### Loop 2: Patient-Driven
```
Patient books session
 ↓ Receives session report
 ↓ Report branded with 24Therapy
 ↓ Family member sees it
 ↓ Family member signs up
```

### Loop 3: Content-Driven
```
Blog post ranks on Google
 ↓ Therapist reads article
 ↓ Therapist tries free tool
 ↓ Converts to paid
 ↓ Therapist shares with community
```
