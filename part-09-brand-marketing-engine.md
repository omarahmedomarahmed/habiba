## BRAND STRATEGY

Most mental health companies market one of two ways:

**Clinical:** Cold, corporate, medical, institutional

**Wellness:** Soft, generic, meditation-focused, lifestyle-oriented

**24Therapy.ai sits between both.** The brand should feel:
- Professional enough for therapists
- Human enough for patients
- Sophisticated enough for investors
- Enterprise enough for healthcare systems

```

  ↓ More Revenue
  ↓ More Therapists
  ↓ More Availability
  ↓ Better Experience
  ↓ More Patients
```
