24Therapy.ai is an AI-native Mental Health Operating System designed to become the global infrastructure layer for therapists, psychologists, psychiatrists, mental health practices, clinics, teletherapy providers, and mental healthcare networks.
The platform combines:

Teletherapy Infrastructure

The long-term vision is not to become a therapy marketplace.

"The Operating System for Mental Healthcare."
Mission
Reduce therapist burnout.
Reduce documentation workload.
Increase patient access.
Improve continuity of care.
Create the world's most advanced mental-health-specific AI infrastructure.

A world where every therapist is assisted by AI.
A world where every patient can access professional mental healthcare within minutes.
A world where mental healthcare records become structured, searchable, secure, and actionable.
A world where therapists spend more time helping patients and less time documenting sessions.
