## Executive Summary

24Therapy.ai is an AI-native Mental Health Operating System designed to become the global infrastructure layer for therapists, psychologists, psychiatrists, mental health practices, clinics, teletherapy providers, and mental healthcare networks.

The platform combines:

- AI Scribe
- AI Clinical Copilot
- Teletherapy Infrastructure
- Patient Management
- Practice Management
- Mental Health CRM
- AI-Assisted Documentation
- Mental Health Analytics
- AI Patient Triage
- Therapist Marketplace
- Mental Health Data Infrastructure

into a single unified platform.

The long-term vision is not to become a therapy marketplace.

The long-term vision is to become:

> **"The Operating System for Mental Healthcare."**

## Market Opportunity

### Therapist Problems

Therapists currently use fragmented tools:
- Zoom / Google Meet / Calendly
- Notion / Google Docs / Excel
- WhatsApp / Paper Notes

Results:
- Documentation is fragmented
- Patient history becomes difficult to track
- Treatment plans become inconsistent
- Medication tracking is often disconnected
- Administrative work creates burnout

### Patient Problems

Patients struggle to:
- Find therapists
- Afford therapists
- Book sessions quickly
- Access therapy anonymously
- Keep records organized
- Understand progress over time

### Industry Problems

- Mental health software is fragmented
- Telehealth platforms lack AI
- AI healthcare platforms lack mental health specialization
- Mental health workflows remain highly manual
