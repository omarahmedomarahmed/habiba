## Core Commercial Philosophy

Most healthcare startups fail because they optimize for:
- Features · Technology · Funding

Instead of:
- Revenue · Retention · Distribution

**24Therapy must be built from Day 1 around a business model that compounds.**

## The Three Businesses Inside 24Therapy

Many founders think they are building one company. In reality, 24Therapy eventually becomes **three companies**.

### Business 1 — AI SaaS
- **Customer:** Therapists
- **Revenue:** Subscriptions · Usage fees · Enterprise contracts

### Business 2 — Marketplace
- **Customer:** Patients
- **Revenue:** Session commissions · Premium placement · Lead generation

### Business 3 — Infrastructure
- **Customer:** Software companies, Clinics, Hospitals, Platforms
- **Revenue:** API usage · White label · Licensing · Enterprise integrations

## Pricing Model

### FREE PLAN
**Goal:** Remove friction
- Patient Profiles · Basic Sessions · Limited AI Minutes · Basic Reports · Basic Storage
- Revenue from: Usage fees + Marketplace commissions

### PROFESSIONAL PLAN
**Target:** Solo Therapists
**Price:** $49–149/month (region-dependent)
- Unlimited Patients · Advanced Reports · AI Copilot
- Patient Timeline · Memory Engine · Analytics · Priority Support

### PRACTICE PLAN
**Target:** Multi-provider organizations
**Pricing:** Per therapist seat
- Assistants · Practice Analytics · Permissions · Team Management
- Shared Billing · Practice Reporting

### ENTERPRISE PLAN
**Target:** Large organizations
**Pricing:** Custom
- SSO · API Access · White Label · Dedicated Support
- Compliance Reviews · Custom Integrations

## Radar Marketplace Economics

One of the largest opportunities.

```
User arrives → Needs help now
            ↓
AI performs intake
            ↓
Radar created
            ↓
Therapists notified
            ↓
Therapist accepts
            ↓
Session begins
            ↓
Platform collects fee
```

### Radar Metrics to Track
- Average Wait Time · Acceptance Rate · Response Rate
- Conversion Rate · Session Revenue · Patient Satisfaction

**Comes after therapist acquisition. Acquire therapists first. Then patients.**

| Channel | Strategy |
|---------|---------|
