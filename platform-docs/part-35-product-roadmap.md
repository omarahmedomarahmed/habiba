# Part 35 — 10-Year Product Roadmap
Quarter-by-Quarter Evolution from AI Scribe to Mental Health Operating System to Mental Health Infrastructure Platform

> **Status:** Outlined in PRD — detailed roadmap coming in future version. High-level trajectory synthesized from throughout the PRD.

---

## The Arc

```
AI Scribe (Year 1)
    ↓
Therapist Copilot (Year 1–2)
    ↓
Mental Health Operating System (Year 2–3)
    ↓
Mental Health Infrastructure Platform (Year 4–5)
    ↓
Behavioral Health Intelligence Layer (Year 5–10)
```
