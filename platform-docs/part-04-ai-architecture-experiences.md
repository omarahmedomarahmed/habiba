# 24Therapy.ai — Part 4: AI Architecture, Therapist Experience, Patient Experience, Radar Marketplace & Revenue Model

---

## Most Important Strategic Decision

24Therapy.ai is NOT an AI therapist.  
24Therapy.ai is NOT a replacement for therapists.

24Therapy.ai exists to:
- Empower therapists
- Reduce burnout
- Reduce documentation burden
- Increase patient access
- Improve continuity of care

**The therapist remains the clinical decision maker. The AI remains an assistant.**

This distinction is critical for:
- Legal compliance · Regulatory compliance · Liability reduction · Clinical trust · Enterprise adoption

---

## AI Architecture — Multi-Agent System

The AI system consists of **multiple specialized agents**, not one model.

### Agent 1 — Conversation Intelligence Agent
**Purpose:** Understand conversations in real time

Responsibilities:
- Speech understanding · Context understanding · Emotion extraction
- Intent detection · Topic segmentation · Relationship tracking · Behavior analysis · Timeline creation

Inputs: Audio · Video Audio · Chat Messages · Patient History · Previous Sessions · Assessments · Medications

Outputs: Conversation Structure · Topics · Risk Flags · Emotional States · Suggested Follow-Ups

---

### Agent 2 — Mental Health Knowledge Agent
**Purpose:** Understand mental health frameworks

Knowledge Sources:
- DSM Frameworks · ICD Frameworks · Evidence-Based Treatments
- Psychotherapy Methodologies · Clinical Research · Medication Databases

Outputs: Treatment Suggestions · Assessment Recommendations · Clinical References · Session Preparation

---

### Agent 3 — Documentation Agent
**Purpose:** Generate documentation

Outputs:
- SOAP Notes · DAP Notes · BIRP Notes · Progress Notes · Insurance Notes
- Treatment Plans · Clinical Summaries · Patient Summaries · Referral Letters · Consultation Reports

---

### Agent 4 — Risk Detection Agent
**Purpose:** Identify safety concerns

Monitors:
- Self-Harm Indicators · Suicidal Language · Violence Indicators
- Substance Abuse Risks · Medication Risks · Severe Behavioral Changes

Produces: Risk Alerts · Escalation Suggestions · Clinical Warnings

> Therapist remains responsible. AI only assists.

---

### Agent 5 — Patient Memory Agent
**Purpose:** Remember long-term patient history

Tracks:
- Symptoms · Events · Relationships · Goals · Progress
- Medication History · Treatment History · Behavioral Patterns · Triggers · Stressors · Achievements

Creates:
- Patient Knowledge Graph · Patient Timeline · Patient Profile Intelligence

---

### Agent 6 — Therapist Copilot Agent
**Purpose:** Assist therapist during sessions

Provides:
- Questions to ask · Topics to explore · Treatment recommendations
- Session summaries · Goal tracking · Progress monitoring

---

### Agent 7 — Radar Matching Agent
**Purpose:** Connect patients to therapists

Inputs: Patient Needs · Urgency · Language · Location · Budget · Specialization · Availability

Outputs: Therapist Rankings · Match Scores · Session Routing

## Radar System — Core Marketplace Engine

**Goal:** Connect patient to therapist within 15 minutes.

```
Patient clicks "Talk To Therapist Now"
              ↓
AI collects: Urgency · Language · Issue Type · Preferred Gender · Budget · Session Type
              ↓
Creates Radar Request
              ↓
All qualified therapists receive alert: App · Push · Email · SMS · WhatsApp (future)
              ↓
First therapist accepts (winner)
              ↓
Video room generated automatically
```

### Therapist Availability Engine

Therapists can set: Available · Busy · Away · Offline · Radar On · Radar Off

AI predicts likely availability.
