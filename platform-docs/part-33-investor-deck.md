## The Story in 8 Acts

### Act 1 — The Problem Is Massive

Mental healthcare is fundamentally broken:
- 1 billion people globally need mental health support
- 75% don't receive it
- Average wait time to see a therapist: 3–6 weeks
- Therapists spend 30–40% of their time on documentation
- No platform connects AI assistance, clinical tools, and marketplace

### Act 2 — Therapists Are Burning Out

- 45-minute therapy session = 45-90 minutes of documentation
- Manual SOAP notes, no AI assistance
- Fragmented tools (calendar + EHR + billing + notes = 5 different apps)
- The best therapists eventually leave private practice

### Act 3 — Patients Can't Access Care

- Expensive: $100–$300/session
- Inaccessible: Weeks-long wait lists
- Stigmatized: Especially in MENA markets
- Digital gap: No modern consumer-grade experience

### Act 4 — 24Therapy Solves Both Sides

**For therapists:** AI Scribe + Copilot + Practice Management
**For patients:** AI Companion + Instant Matching (Radar) + Marketplace

One platform. Both sides. Network effects.

### Act 5 — The AI Moat

Unlike simple AI note-takers:
- **Clinical Memory Engine** — longitudinal patient understanding
- **Proprietary RAG** — retrieval across all patient data
- **Data Flywheel** — every session makes the AI smarter
- **10 Specialized Agents** — purpose-built for mental health

The moat compounds over time.
