# 24Therapy.ai — Part 18: Brand Strategy, Website Architecture, Messaging System, Category Creation, Sales Funnels, Marketing Engine & Public Narrative

## THE BIGGEST MARKETING MISTAKE

Most founders market features. **Customers buy outcomes.**

Therapists do not wake up wanting:
- AI
- Transcription
- Vector databases
- Knowledge graphs
- Large language models

They want:
- Less paperwork
- Better organization
- More patient time
- Better memory
- Better outcomes
- More revenue
- Less burnout

**24Therapy messaging must focus on outcomes.**
